Join Our Team as a Web Developer

About Us

PEC Burger is one of the Spanish brands with the fastest online growth and international projection. We are a young and dynamic team, as well as digital natives with a clear focus on scalability and work by objectives.

Job Description: What will be your mission?

  • Translate technical requirements into technical solutions.
  • Create from scratch new Front end and Back end features with React and C#, NetCore.
  • Be part of the decision making regarding Front end and Back end Architecture and new features design.
  • Assure the delivery and the quality of your code.
  • Combine your work with other teams and departments.

Requirements: What profile are we looking for?

  • Full stack Engineer with at least 4-5 years of experience in the same role.
  • At least 3 years of experience with React.js and C#. Experience with other programming languages will be appreciated but not necessary.
  • Organized, detail-oriented person.
  • Positive attitude. Influential Profile.
  • Orientation to work in a team.
  • We are eager to learn, enjoy, contribute new ideas and grow together!

Job Details: What do we offer?

Location: Hybrid modality (Spain). Would have no objection to travel to Europe in the future). Contract: Indefinite full-time. Laptop and Display. Annual Salary Reviews. Flexibility. Career plan in the company marked by objectives. We offer a good working environment, flexibility and a career plan based on objectives - we want to grow together! Free Health insurance.

How to Apply for the Position?

Send your CV to peccareer@pec.com indicating that you apply to the offer WEB DEVELOPER and tell us why you would love to be part of PEC BURGER.
